24 hour Emergency Call Outs




We've all been there.
AHHHH!! I have to stay late at work and no one is home to feed Rover!
OR
AHHHH!! My car broke down and my friend watching Fluffy has to leave at 5!

At Lil Pal, we know $%*! happens.
Existing customers can call us 24 hours a day for Emergency assistance with feeds, walks, vet appointments or even overnights.
If you have an unpredicatable schedule, maybe consider our 24-hour Emergency Retainer Subscription to avoid shrt-notice callout fees if you expect them to happen often.
Otherwise, 24-hour emergency callouts are charged a flat rate per callout, plus the rate for the service being requested on short notice fees. Other fees may apply, please ask our staff when you call for an estimate on specific callout fees.
You will be required to pay the initial callout flat fee at time of request, before service is fulfilled.
